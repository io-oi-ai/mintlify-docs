## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [io-oi.ai](https://io-oi.ai) and create your first project. A project is the container for all your documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your first document">
    Open the editor, type your content, or paste in existing text. Use the AI assistant to refine the language and structure automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite collaborators">
    Add teammates to your project so they can review, comment on, and edit documents together in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Hit **Publish** to make your documentation live. Share the link or connect a custom domain from your project settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
